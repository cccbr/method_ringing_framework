Appendix G. Version 2 Development

A.
Team

Version 2 was developed by the CC Framework Team, which is part of the Technical and Taxonomy CC Workgroup.

The CC Framework team comprises Tim Barnes (team leader), Mark Davies, John Harrison, Graham John, Don Morrison and Philip Saddleton.

B.
Objectives

Version 2 was developed to address:
(a) Suggestions received from members of the ringing community subsequent to the implementation of version 1; and
(b) Improvements identified by the Framework team.
