2. Editions

Edition 1
Implemented February 24, 2019 with an effective date of June 1, 2019.
Edition 2
Implemented January 26, 2022 with an effective date of May 1, 2022.
Edition 2 is the current live version of the Framework.
Edition 3
This is the working draft for Edition 3.
The term "Edition" replaces "Version" in Edition 3 to better reflect that each Framework edition is an authorised replacement for earlier editions. The text of Editions 1 and 2 remains unchanged from their original publication as Version 1 and Version 2 respectively.
See Appendix G for information on the development of Edition 3 and the changes that were made.
See Appendix H for information on the ringing community consultation that tookplace prior to the implementation of Edition 3.
PDF documents
All editions of the Framework are available as PDF documents. Each edition has three volumes:
  • A Framework volume containing the main body of the Framework without notes and examples, suitable for quick reference.
  • A Framework with notes volume containing the main body with all notes and examples shown.
  • A Framework appendices with notes volume containing the appendices with all notes and examples shown.
